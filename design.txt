


    
    ServiHub – Complete Design &amp; Brand Guidelines

    

    
    
    1. Brand Overview

    
    Brand Name

    ServiHub

    
    
    Brand Meaning

    ServiHub represents a centralized digital hub connecting clients with multiple service-based businesses through a unified booking marketplace.

    
    
    Brand Personality

    Professional

    Modern SaaS

    Trustworthy

    Scalable

    Friendly and approachable

    

    
    
    
    2. Brand Taglines

    Primary Tagline: “Your Hub for Service Bookings”

    Alternative Taglines: - “Connecting Clients with Services” - “One Platform. Endless Services.” - “Where Services Meet Scheduling”

    

    
    
    3. Logo Design

    
    Logo Concept

    The ServiHub logo represents connection and centralization through a hub-and-node visual system.

    
    Symbol Meaning

    Center Circle = Platform Core

    Outer Nodes = Businesses

    Connection Lines = Marketplace Ecosystem
